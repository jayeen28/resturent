About Me

I am a chef, food writer, food developer, tv host.Some of my fondest early memories were of my grandmother, over a stove, fixing food for our daily feast. At age nine I knew I loved being in the kitchen and helping out my grandmother. My grandmother, an amazing cook, had passed many family recipes down to me. I am so lucky to show off some of the family recipes and to be able to recreate my own adaptation according to the taste of locals and foreigners of today.
